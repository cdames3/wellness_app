# Wellness App

The Wellness App is designed to help users improve their overall well-being through various features that promote mental and physical health. The app offers:

- Guided meditations
- Fitness tracking
- Nutrition advice
- Mood journaling
- Community support

## Features:

### Guided Meditations
Users can access a variety of guided meditation sessions tailored to different needs, such as stress relief, focus, and sleep.

### Fitness Tracking
Track your daily physical activities, set fitness goals, and monitor progress over time.

### Nutrition Advice
Receive personalized nutrition plans and tips to maintain a balanced diet.

### Mood Journaling
Keep track of your emotions and thoughts through daily journaling prompts.

### Community Support
Connect with others on a similar journey and provide mutual support through forums and messaging.

## Getting Started

To get started with the Wellness App, simply download it from the app store and create an account. Explore the various features and begin your journey to a healthier lifestyle!
